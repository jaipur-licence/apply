Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1 Licence kitne dino me banta hai?

Learning licence turant mil jaata hai, aur aapke permanent driving licence ke liye ek mahina lagta hai.

2 Kya aap car chalana sikhate hain?

Haan, hum car chalana sikhate hain. Poore jaipur me suvidha milegi

3 Kya aap fees lenge?

Haan, hum aapko madad aur salah dene ke liye ek chhoti si fees lenge. Humari koshish aapko behtar service dena hai.

4 Kya aap licence renew karte hain?

Haan, Jaipur me hum driving licence ka renewal bhi karte hain. Agar aapka licence expire ho gaya ho, toh hum aapki madad karenge.

5 Kya aap aur RTO kaam bhi karte hain?

Haan, hum RTO se related saare kaam karte hain jaise new driving licence, licence renew, gaadi transfer, Gaadi ka Insurance, Manpsand Number dilwana.
